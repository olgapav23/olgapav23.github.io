Research Word - Motion

Some of this information is from the google alerts that I set and some that I found by simple Google search.
I looked into more information on importance of motion/movement in interaction design. The first thing I found this week was the video originally posted on lynda.com and part of longer series about interaction design, but basically talks about ways people respond to motion,- Interaction design: How people respond to motion. Some of the important points mentioned in this video:

  • Motion attracts attention, therefore can be used to direct it
  • We can direct audiences attention to hide and show information(dropdown menus, ads, pop-ups, fading in and out)
  • Also associations can be created through motion. Helps to identify relationships or connections
  • Can also lead to distraction
  • Such motion as refreshing our browser can cause distraction and lead to losing focus and not uderstanding the differences that occured after refreshing
  • Video is a pimary source of motion in interactive design today
  • To enhance user experience some cosiderations must be dealt with when using video in your design:
    1. Avoid auto-play
    2. Provide media and audio control
    3. Provide progress and status information

Another thing that I stumbled upon is the "Design in motion. The new frontier of interaction design" by Antonio De Pasquale where he discusses the common qualities and similar parts between Interaction and Motion.
Important points from the presentation:

  • Interaction >> Communication >> Motion
